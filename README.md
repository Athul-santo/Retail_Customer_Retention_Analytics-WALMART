Retail Customer Retention Analytics – Walmart

Project Overview:
Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze Walmart customer behavior by consolidating demographics, purchase history, store/e-commerce performance, and loyalty data. The dashboard segments high-value, repeat, and churned customers, providing actionable insights to improve retention, loyalty engagement, and regional strategies.

Key Features:

Churn & Retention Metrics: Visualize churn by region, income, channel, and loyalty tier.
Repeat Purchase Analysis: Segment customers by purchase frequency and analyze top product categories.
Promotion & Loyalty Impact: Measure effect of promotions and loyalty tiers on retention.
Store & Channel Performance: Compare transaction amounts and churn rates by store type and region.
Customer Lifetime Value (CLV): Segment customers into high/low CLV and analyze trends over time.
Interactive Dashboard: Multi-page Power BI report with slicers for Region, Channel, Income, and Loyalty Tier.

Technologies Used:
Power BI, DAX, Power Query, CSV Data Processing

Objective:
Enable Walmart to identify at-risk customers, optimize loyalty programs, and strengthen retention strategies through data-driven insights.
